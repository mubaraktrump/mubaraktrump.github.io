How to Buy

  • 1
    Download the MetaMask extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring BNB from an exchange or another wallet.
  • 2
    Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) PancakeSwap. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet". Then select MetaMask
  • 3
    To swap BNB for $Trump , select BNB in the "from" field and choose $Trump in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.
  • 4
    Verify your transaction status in MetaMask under the "Activity" tab or on BscScan using your BSC address. You import the token to your MetaMask by pasting in the CA.
